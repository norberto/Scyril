App Mockup

Home page

At the home page you can find the summary of the app. Your latest picks, favorite songs, top songs and everything else can be found on this page.

Selection menu

Most important things can be reached through this page. You can change your settings, access your profile, check favorite songs, browse other songs and check the home page with ease.

Favorites

All your favorite picks are on this page, you can follow any song you like and reach it later in a few taps.

Search

Search! If there is a song you know, you can search it up using this option for that. Had those lyrics in your head all day but can't remember the title? It works with that too.

Lyrics

The most inportant thing of the app. Lyrics. You can enable auto-scroll option, speed it up or just scroll with your finger. Karaoke any way you like!

Browse songs

At the browse page - today's hits, popular songs, pop music, all the songs you want can be found on this page. Know a song you'd like? That is fine, use search option. Don't know what you want? Browse!
